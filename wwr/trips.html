Family Float

A relaxed scenic trip designed for beginners and families. Enjoy gentle rapids, basic paddling instruction, and plenty of time to take in the views.

Canyon Explorer

A balanced adventure with exciting rapids and breathtaking canyon scenery. Perfect for groups who want more action while still feeling comfortable.

  • Difficulty: Class II–III
  • Duration: 4–5 hours
  • Minimum age: 12+

Extreme Rapids

High-intensity whitewater for thrill-seekers. Expect big waves, faster currents, and technical lines guided by our most experienced team.

  • Difficulty: Class III–IV+
  • Duration: Full day
  • Minimum age: 16+
